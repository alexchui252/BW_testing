About us

What we do...

Hey there! At BioinformaticsWorkflows, we aim to be a comprehensive resource for researchers at every level to come and leanr more about the field of bioinformatics, from the math and science principles that make bioinformatics possible as well as how to implement code for your specific project

If you're interested in working with us, have suggestions for our content, or just want to have a chat, fill out the contact form at the bottom of the page! Besides providing educational resources, we also want to facilitate connections between researchers that could become fruitful collaborations!
